# Complementary Guidance Systems — Expanded Catalog v2

## Functional Layers (the app mechanic)

1. **Blueprint** (birth date only) — your fixed chart: natal astrology, HD, numerology core numbers
2. **Personal cycles** (birth date × today) — where *you* are in *your* unfolding timeline
3. **Cosmic weather** (today only) — same for everyone: panchang, lunar phase, Tong Shu
4. **On-demand** — divination the user initiates

---

## Personal Cycle / Time-Lord Systems

*The "birth date × today" layer — the core app mechanic.*

- **Vimshottari Dasha** (Vedic) — nested planetary periods (mahadasha → antardasha → pratyantar...) resolvable down to the day; the most developed personal-cycle system anywhere
- **Annual profections** (Hellenistic) — each year of life activates one house and a "lord of the year"
- **Zodiacal releasing** — Hellenistic time-lords from the Lots; four nested levels reaching daily granularity
- **Firdaria** — Persian planetary periods, 75-year cycle
- **BaZi luck pillars** — 10-year cycles, plus annual/monthly/daily pillar interactions with the day master
- **Solar return chart** — annual chart cast for the birthday; **lunar returns** — monthly version
- **Secondary progressions & solar arc** — the natal chart slowly evolving; progressed Moon cycle (~28 years)
- **Numerology pinnacles, challenges, personal year → month → day** — fully algorithmic, perfect for an app
- **Cards of Destiny / Destiny Cards** — birth date maps to a playing card; each year divides into seven 52-day planetary periods with their own cards; practically designed for this exact app mechanic
- **Javanese Weton** — birth combination of the 7-day and 5-day (Pasaran) weeks recurs every 35 days as a personal power day
- **Balinese Pawukon** — 210-day calendar of ten concurrent week-systems; *otonan* = your 210-day "birthday"
- **Climacteric years** — the classical Greek system of critical life years (multiples of 7 and 9)
- **Biorhythms** — 23/28/33-day physical/emotional/intellectual cycles

## Asian Systems

- **Zi Wei Dou Shu / Tử Vi Đẩu Số** — "Purple Star" astrology, 12 palaces from lunar birth data; the dominant system in Vietnam, annual readings are a cultural institution
- **Nine Star Ki (Kyūsei Kigaku)** — Japanese nine-star system with yearly/monthly/daily star movements and travel directions
- **Korean Saju** — Korean four-pillars variant
- **Sexagenary day cycle (60 jiazi)** — every day has a stem-branch signature, unbroken count for ~3,000 years; the engine under BaZi and Tong Shu
- **12 Day Officers (Jian Chu)** — the rotating cycle inside the Tong Shu that labels each day's quality
- **Choghadiya** — Vedic division of day/night into 8 auspicious/inauspicious slots each; plus **Disha Shool** (don't-travel direction per weekday) and **Hora** (Vedic planetary hours)
- **Hindu weekday system** — each day ruled by a deity with its own vrat (fast), color, gemstone, mantra (Somvar/Shiva, Mangalvar/Hanuman...)
- **Burmese Mahabote** — chart built from day-of-week of birth (8-day week, Wednesday split in two)
- **Thai birth-day system** — day of week at birth determines color, Buddha posture, and lucky/unlucky days
- **Tibetan Buddhist practice calendar** — Medicine Buddha day (8th), Guru Rinpoche day (10th), Dakini day (25th), protector day (29th), Sojong on new/full moon — a complete monthly practice scaffold
- **Japanese lucky days beyond Rokuyō** — Ichiryū Manbai-bi, Tensha-bi, and the broader tradition of **Onmyōdō**

## Structural Notes for the App

### Input matrix

| Input | What it unlocks |
|---|---|
| Birth date only | Blueprint charts, fixed signatures, personal number archetypes |
| Birth date + today | Personal cycles, dasha periods, profections, Weton, personal day numbers |
| Today only | Panchang, lunar phase, Tong Shu, geomagnetic data, Sabian symbol |
| User-initiated | Divination: I Ching, tarot, runes |

### Cadence layers

`Hourly` → `Daily` → `Lunar month` → `Season` → `Year` → `Decade` → `Era`

Each system occupies a different slot — which is why they complement rather than contradict each other.

### Strongest candidates for the core daily loop

These systems are *literally built* on birth date × current date → daily output:

- Vimshottari Dasha
- Annual profections
- Cards of Destiny
- Javanese Weton
- Dreamspell / 13-Moon
- Numerology personal day number
- Zi Wei Dou Shu (Tử Vi)
